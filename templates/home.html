Airbnb Rentals for 10 Major European Cities

Project Description:

For this project we will be analyzing Airbnb data in some of the most popular European cities(Amsterdam, Athens, Barcelona, Berlin, Budapest, Lisbon, London, Paris, Rome, & Vienna). The data includes columns for various attributes such as nightly price, room types, cleanliness ratings and satisfaction ratings, bedrooms, distance from the city center, and more to capture an in-depth understanding of Airbnb prices on weekdays vs weekends in the provided European cities.
